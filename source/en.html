Oil price discovery based on futures price – global standard

  • Standard mechanism in the financial markets: Central counterparty, clearing, risk management system based on SPAN© methodology
  • Futures expire with physical delivery of the commodity ensuring convergence between paper and physical market
  • Exchange price discovery is based on the balance of supply and demand from a significant number of participants on a single platform. The mechanism determines the fair price for the commodity
  • The price is fixed by the rules of the largest Russian commodity exchange
  • Futures is a standardized contract, all terms of which are well-known
  • The futures market is regulated by the Bank of Russia
